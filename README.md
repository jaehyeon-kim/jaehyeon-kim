## About Me

[![Linkedin Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/-LinkedIn-blue?style=flat&logo=Linkedin&logoColor=white&link=https://www.linkedin.com/in/jaehyeon-kim-76b93429/)](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jaehyeon-kim-76b93429/)

I lead Developer Experience at Factor House, where I create technical content, drive platform engineering initiatives, and present at meetups and conferences. With expertise in real-time systems and modern data platforms, I've worked extensively with Kafka, Flink, Spark, and the integrated architectures. As a passionate engineer and writer, I share practical insights on real-time analytics, data architectures, data lakehouse patterns, data lineage, and building systems that remain resilient, observable, and maintainable at scale.

Learn more about Factor House's work: [https://factorhouse.io/](https://factorhouse.io/)

Also, I explore topics related to Kafka, Flink, and streaming data ecosystems on my blog: [https://jaehyeon.me](https://jaehyeon.me).
